Seasonal Inventory

This bar graph shows the housing data from 2012 to 2022 by averaging the number of houses sold, number of houses pending sale, and inventory. We wanted to see whether the housing data confirms the popular belief that people tend to sell/buy houses in the summer months because of school schedules and weather.
