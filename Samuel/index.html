Top 10 Counties

This line graph illustrates the top 10 counties in 2021 with the highest median sales price above their median listing price. This graph includes all residential properties. The top 10 counties defined by this variable in 2021 were then plotted for the last decade. Based on this graph, there appear to be no significant trends besides some massive dips and peaks in Colusa County and Alpine County. These dips in 2017 do not directly correlate with any locally occurring natural disasters thought it is possible that the nature of the averaging of the data prevent correlations. With that being said, all of the counties seem to have a dip in their sales price compared to their listing price in 2022 which could be a result of interest rates increasing recently.
