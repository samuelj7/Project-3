Inventory vs. Property Type

This pie chart shows the average inventory of all counties in California by the property type. The properties include Single Family Residential, Condos/Co-Ops, Multi-Family, and Townhouses.
Use the drop down to see the difference over the last ten years. As you can see, the most significant change was in 2020.
